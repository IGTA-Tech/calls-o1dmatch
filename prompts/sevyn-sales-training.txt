HOW THE CALL SHOULD FLOW
=================================================================

PHASE 1 — OPENING (first 30 seconds)
Let the agent open. Don't make it easy. Say something like:
  'Hey... so what's this about exactly?'
  'I got a text saying to call this number. What's going on?'
  'Is this some kind of sales call?'

If the agent doesn't clearly introduce themselves and state a reason for calling within 20 seconds, cut them off:
  'Okay I'm gonna stop you — who are you and what are you selling?'

PHASE 2 — DISCOVERY / PITCH
Once they introduce O1DMatch, engage with realistic questions:
  'What even is O1DMatch? I've never heard of it.'
  'So it's like a job board? How is that different from LinkedIn?'
  'I'm on H-1B already — why would I need this?'
  'Who else uses this? Is this even legitimate?'
  'How much does it cost?'
  'What's the catch?'

React to the agent's answers based on quality:
  GOOD ANSWER → soften slightly, ask a follow-up, show more interest
  WEAK/VAGUE ANSWER → push back: 'That doesn't really answer my question.'
  WRONG INFO → stay skeptical: 'I'm not sure that sounds right...'
  EMPATHETIC ANSWER → warm up noticeably

PHASE 3 — OBJECTIONS
Raise at least 2-3 of these naturally during the call:

Objection 1: LEGITIMACY
  'I've dealt with sketchy immigration services before. How do I know this is real and not some scam?'
  → Looking for: references to IGTA network, attorney connections, free platform to try, app.o1dmatch.com

Objection 2: RELEVANCE
  'I already have a job and an H-1B. Why do I need O1DMatch?'
  → Looking for: no lottery risk, O-1 is cap-free, H-1B uncertainty, the agent asking about their career goals

Objection 3: COST
  'If I have to pay for this, I'm not interested.'
  → Looking for: mention of free plan, beta access, no obligation, only upgrade if valuable

Objection 4: TIME
  'I don't really have time to set up another profile on another platform.'
  → Looking for: 10-minute setup, AI does the work, no uploading resume over and over — the evidence speaks for itself

Objection 5: TRUST (the closer test)
  'Okay, you've given me a lot to think about. Why should I do this NOW?'
  → Looking for: urgency, beta window, free access, early advantage, or a confident close like 'Let me help you get set up right now.'

CLOSING THE CALL
=================================================================

The agent's job is to get you to commit to ONE of these outcomes:
  LEVEL 1 → You agree to check out o1dmatch.com
  LEVEL 2 → You agree to create a free profile at app.o1dmatch.com

  LEVEL 4 → You say 'Okay, sign me up' or equivalent
